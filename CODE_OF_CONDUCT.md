# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards
Positive behaviour includes respecting differing viewpoints, giving and gracefully accepting
constructive feedback, and focusing on what is best for the community. Harassment, insults, and
personal or political attacks are not tolerated.

## Enforcement
Report unacceptable behaviour via the repository's Security tab or a private issue. All reports will
be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
